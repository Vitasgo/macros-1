The body macros will format the main body text into the IEEE two column standard.

The attributes of the IEEE two column body features:
Font: 
Times New Roman

Columns: 
Two 3.5” columns with 0.25” spacing

Margins:
.75”, 1”
.63”, 63”

References > Bib:
Chicago


Paragraph spacing:
custom paragraph spacing, alignment Left.

Text alignment:
"Justify"

To initiate the macro, simply highlight the body text, then under tools, choose macros, and run Body.
